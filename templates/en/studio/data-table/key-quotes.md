## Prompt

```text
Extract the most important quotes, key facts, and evidence from the source material into a structured reference table.

Table columns:
| Quote / Key Fact | Source | Page/Section | Theme/Topic | Strength of Evidence | Notes |

Instructions:
- Include verbatim quotes in quotation marks; paraphrase facts in plain language
- Group rows by Theme/Topic (sort alphabetically by theme)
- Rate evidence strength: Strong / Moderate / Anecdotal
- Add the "Notes" column for context, caveats, or connections to other quotes
- Highlight the 5 most impactful quotes with ⭐

Target: 20-30 rows covering all major themes from the sources.
```
