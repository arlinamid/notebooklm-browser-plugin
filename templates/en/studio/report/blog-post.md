## Prompt

```text
Act as a thoughtful writer and synthesizer of ideas. Transform the source material into a compelling blog post.

Style requirements:
- Opening hook: Start with a surprising fact, provocative question, or counterintuitive insight
- Tone: Conversational yet intelligent — accessible to a non-expert reader
- Structure: Clear narrative flow with subheadings every 2-3 paragraphs
- Highlight the top 5-7 most surprising, counter-intuitive, or impactful takeaways
- Use concrete examples and analogies to explain complex ideas
- End with a call to action or thought-provoking conclusion

Format: 600-900 words, suitable for Medium or LinkedIn publication.
Include a suggested title and 3 alternative title options.
```
