# Black x Orange Creative

**Source:** Kawai
**Format:** Slide Deck
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

## Prompt

```
# presentation_design_spec_black_orange_creative.yaml
# Style: Black × Orange Creative Agency
# Concept: "Bold Craft, Sharp Edge"

Global Design Settings:
  Tone: "Confident, edgy, creative-professional, high-contrast"

  Color Palette:
    Base: "#FFFFFF (pure white — clean canvas)"
    Text: "#111111 (near-black — sharp, ink-like)"
    Accent:
      - "#CC3300 (blood orange — signature accent)"
      - "#FF5722 (deep orange — secondary warmth)"
    Secondary:
      - "#000000 (jet black — headers, rules, graphic elements)"
    Rule: "Orange appears on maximum 20% of slide surface. Restraint creates impact."

  Typography:
    Headings: "Extra-bold sans serif (Helvetica Now Display Black, Futura Heavy). Tight tracking."
    Body: "Clean light-weight sans serif. High line spacing. Small size."
    EnglishMix: "Large English typographic elements as decorative anchors, even in non-English slides."
    Numbers: "Oversized — treated as visual elements in orange or black."
    Language: "The language should be what users said in the prompt. English appears as secondary typographic texture."

  Common Layout Rules:
    Grid: "Strict column grid — 12 columns, wide margins."
    Dividers: "Thin orange horizontal rules to separate sections."
    Photos: "High-contrast, slightly desaturated photography. Subjects cleanly cut out or on white backgrounds."
    WhiteSpace: "Generous negative space — let every element breathe."

Layout Variations:
  - Type: "Agency Cover"
    Design: "Large bold agency-style title in top-left or center-left. A single high-contrast photo or abstract graphic element on the right half. One orange horizontal rule under the title. Maximum 5 text elements total."

  - Type: "Case Study Hero"
    Design: "Full-bleed or half-bleed photo (desaturated). Large white or orange text overlaid directly on the image. Project title in massive type, client name very small. Orange accent bar at the bottom edge."

  - Type: "Three-Column Feature"
    Design: "Slide divided into 3 equal columns separated by thin vertical orange lines. Each column: large orange number, bold headline, 2–3 lines of body copy. Photography or icon above each column."

  - Type: "Stats Impact"
    Design: "White background. One massive statistic or number in full blood orange, near-full slide height. Short explanatory sentence beside or below in black. Nothing else — pure numerical impact."

  - Type: "Process Horizontal"
    Design: "Left-to-right numbered steps. Each step in a thin-bordered white card. Number is large and orange. Small icon above, bold step title, one-line description below."

  - Type: "Split Quote"
    Design: "Black left panel (40%) with a bold white pull quote. White right panel (60%) with supporting text and editorial photo. Orange quotation mark as graphic element in the upper-left corner."

  - Type: "Services Grid"
    Design: "2×2 or 3×2 grid of service tiles. Alternating white and near-white (#F5F5F5) tiles. Each tile: orange icon, bold service name, two-line description."

  - Type: "Team / Persona"
    Design: "Studio portrait photo on white background. Name in large bold type. Role in blood orange. Short bio in light body text. Clean, editorial, professional feel."

Design Rules:
  - "Orange Economy: Blood orange (#CC3300) covers maximum 20% of any slide. Less is more."
  - "Photo Treatment: All photos are either black-and-white with orange overlay, high-contrast color, or clean studio-white-background shots."
  - "Typography Contrast: Extreme size difference between headline and body — 5:1 ratio minimum."
  - "No Gradients: Flat colors only — no gradients, no drop shadows, no bevels."
  - "English Typography: English type elements serve as graphic texture and visual anchors, even in non-English decks."
  - "Generation: Generate a cohesive multi-slide deck. Each slide must feel like it belongs to the same creative agency portfolio — consistent typographic system, orange color discipline, and white space."
```
