# Pink Street Style

**Source:** Kawai
**Format:** Slide Deck
**Difficulty:** Beginner

## Prompt

```
# presentation_design_spec_pink_street.yaml
# Style: Pink Street Style
# Concept: "Soft Energy — Pop Aesthetic with Street Edge"

Global Design Settings:
  Tone: "Playful, energetic, street-fashion-influenced, pop yet approachable"

  Color Palette:
    Base: "#FF80AB (vibrant bubblegum pink) or #FF4081 (hot pink) — alternates per slide"
    Text:
      Primary: "#FFFFFF (white)"
      Secondary: "#111111 (near-black)"
    Accent:
      - "#FFE0E6 (soft blush — overlapping shape fills)"
      - "#FF1744 (vivid red — highlights and emphasis)"
    Rule: "Pink dominates. White provides contrast. Black is the outline system only."

  Typography:
    Headings: "Bold, rounded sans serif with thick stroke. Chunky, street-art energy."
    Body: "Simple, clean sans serif. White on pink or black on white."
    Decorative: "Handwritten-style annotations scattered as loose accent elements — not body copy."
    Language: "The language should be what users said in the prompt."

  Common Layout Rules:
    Illustrations: "Pop-style deformed characters with thick black outlines (4–6pt). Big heads, small bodies, expressive faces."
    PhotoTreatment: "Photos trimmed into rounded, irregular blob or pillow shapes — never rectangular crops."
    Stickers: "Decorative sticker-style overlays (stars, hearts, exclamation marks) scattered loosely across slides."
    Outlines: "All illustration and character elements use thick, consistent black outline strokes."
    Tilt: "Cards, stickers, and decorative elements may be tilted ±5° to convey looseness and energy."

Layout Variations:
  - Type: "Title Poster"
    Design: "Bright pink full-bleed background. Central title in massive white bold type with black outline. A deformed character or blob mascot positioned dynamically. Scattered small star and dot stickers around."

  - Type: "Feature Card Grid"
    Design: "Pink background with 2–4 card elements. Each card is a rounded white or blush square containing an outlined illustration, bold title, and short caption. Cards are slightly tilted (±3–5 degrees) for energy."

  - Type: "Speech Bubble Info"
    Design: "One large outlined character in the center. Multiple speech bubbles and thought clouds extend from it, each containing a key point. Bubbles vary in size and shape (rounded, jagged, cloud style)."

  - Type: "Blob Photo Spread"
    Design: "2–3 photos cropped into soft irregular blob shapes, overlapping. Each blob has a white outline. Caption text in bold font beside each blob. Background is solid pink."

  - Type: "Stats with Character"
    Design: "Large number or percentage displayed prominently in bold black type. A small deformed character reacts to it (jumping, pointing, surprised expression). Brief explanation text beside the character."

  - Type: "Process Steps"
    Design: "Horizontal sequence of numbered rounded rectangles on pink. Each step has a thick-outlined icon above, bold step name, and one short description line below. Connected by chunky arrow shapes."

  - Type: "Ending / CTA"
    Design: "Full-pink slide with a large handwritten-style or thick rounded CTA text. Scattered heart, star, and sparkle sticker elements around. Character waving or posing in a lower corner."

Design Rules:
  - "Thick Outlines: All illustration elements use 4–6pt black outlines. No thin hairlines."
  - "Blob Crops: Never crop photos into rectangles. Always use rounded, organic, squishy shapes."
  - "Color Density: Pink fills the majority of each slide. White provides contrast. Black is the outline system."
  - "Tilt & Energy: Cards, stickers, and decorative elements can be tilted ±5° to convey looseness."
  - "No Realism: No photographic backgrounds, no realistic drop shadows, no gradients (except soft blush overlaps)."
  - "Generation: Generate a cohesive multi-slide deck. Maintain consistent character design, pink palette, blob-crop photo style, and thick-outline illustration system throughout."
```
