## Prompt

```text
Create exam-preparation flashcards on the topic: [TOPIC].

Prioritize:
1. High-frequency exam topics and testable facts
2. Key definitions and terminology
3. Important dates, names, formulas, or statistics
4. Common misconceptions to avoid
5. Compare/contrast pairs

Card format:
Q: [clear, exam-style question]
A: [precise answer with key detail]

Generate 20 cards ordered by importance. Mark the 5 most critical with ⭐.
```
