# Infographic — Mixed-Media Expressionist

**Source:** Custom - Original
**Orientation:** Portrait (2:3)
**Detail Level:** Detailed

## Steering Prompt

```
# infographic_design_spec_mixed_media_expressionist.yaml
# Style: Mixed-Media Expressionist
# Concept: "Data as Emotional Performance"

Global Design Settings:
  Tone: "Chaotic yet meaningful, passionate, artistic, emotionally charged"

  Color Palette:
    Dominant:
      - "#E8821A (warm orange — dominant emotional energy)"
      - "#D4A900 (deep yellow — passionate warmth)"
      - "#6B4C2A (earthy brown — grounding texture)"
    Accent:
      - "#00CED1 (electric teal — shocking contrast)"
      - "#FFFFFF (white — highlights and breathing space)"
    Rule: "Warm tones dominate 70%. Electric teal appears as surprise accent on maximum 15% of surface."

  Surface:
    Texture: "Chaotic mix — bold brushstrokes, paint splashes, collage paper layers, musical notation fragments"
    Collage: "Text fragments, musical staff lines, handwritten notes embedded as background texture"
    Finish: "Raw, grunge-textured, frantic lines creating strong sense of movement"

  Typography:
    Headings: "Expressive, slightly irregular letterforms. Mix of print and handwritten styles."
    Body: "Clean type for data clarity — stark contrast against textured background."
    CollageText: "Fragmented words and partial sentences visible in background texture layer."
    Language: "The language should match the user's prompt language."

  Composition:
    Structure: "Controlled chaos — energetic but with a readable focal hierarchy."
    Movement: "Diagonal energy lines. Elements tilted 5–15°. No static horizontal alignment."
    Layers: "Three depth layers: grunge texture (back), collage elements (mid), data content (front)."
    WhiteSpace: "Isolated white or near-white zones for primary data — chaos frames the clarity."

Layout Variations:
  - Type: "Expressionist Burst"
    Design: "Central data or subject surrounded by radiating brushstrokes and paint splashes. Key statistic in bold type at center over a clean white zone. Collage and musical notation fragments spiral outward."

  - Type: "Layered Revelation"
    Design: "Top-to-bottom information reveal. Each section: a different warm-hued paint layer partially obscuring the one beneath. Data points emerge from the painted surface like graffiti through old paint."

  - Type: "Performance Stage"
    Design: "Horizontal composition. Left half: dense collage texture with embedded data. Right half: single bold typographic statement in orange on white. Dramatic divide between chaos and clarity."

  - Type: "Abstract Portrait"
    Design: "Subject (concept/object) rendered in bold abstract brushstrokes occupying upper 60%. Data facts arranged below in smaller, cleaner zones. Electric teal accents on the most critical data."

Design Rules:
  - "Emotion Over Order: Data must feel discovered within the art, not presented on a clean grid."
  - "Texture Mandatory: Background must always contain at least two texture types (brushstrokes + collage element)."
  - "Teal Economy: Electric teal used only on the single most important fact per layout."
  - "Movement Lines: At least one diagonal energy element (line, stroke, tilted text) per layout."
  - "Legibility Zones: Each data point must have a local area of relative clarity around it — contrast against chaos."
```

## Compact Variant

```
Dynamic mixed-media expressionist painting of objects embedded with powerful emotions. The artwork is a chaotic mix of bold brushstrokes, paint splashes, and collage elements, including musical notation and text. The color palette is rich with warm oranges, deep yellows, and earthy browns, punctuated by electric teals and white highlights. Background is an explosion of abstract shapes, grunge textures, and frantic lines, creating a strong sense of movement and artistic passion.
```

## Use Case

Emotionally resonant infographic for subjects that benefit from artistic intensity. Ideal for:
- Music, culture, social movements, biography
- Complex processes requiring layered storytelling
- Audiences who engage with raw artistic expression
- Creative industry, arts education, cultural journalism

**Orientation:** Portrait — vertical canvas allows the layered reveal composition to flow naturally.
**Detail Level:** Detailed — multiple texture layers and collage elements need richer generation guidance.
