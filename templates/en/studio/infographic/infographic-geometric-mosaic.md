# Infographic — Geometric Mosaic

**Source:** Custom - Original
**Orientation:** Portrait (2:3)
**Detail Level:** Standard

## Steering Prompt

```
# infographic_design_spec_geometric_mosaic.yaml
# Style: Geometric Mosaic — Stained Glass
# Concept: "Sacred Geometry Meets Data"

Global Design Settings:
  Tone: "Rich, decorative, contemporary abstract realism, handcrafted artistic finish"

  Color Palette:
    Dominant:
      - "#1A3A6B (deep blue — structural anchor)"
      - "#E87722 (warm orange — focal energy)"
      - "#D4A017 (amber — rich mid-tone)"
      - "#C8860A (ochre — earthy warmth)"
    Neutral:
      - "#8C7B6B (muted warm gray — balancing tone)"
      - "#F5EDD6 (warm off-white — light panels)"
    Rule: "High contrast between deep blue and warm oranges. Muted neutrals provide visual rest between vivid panels."

  Surface:
    Texture: "Painterly acrylic or oil on wood — visible brush strokes, subtle wood grain"
    Edges: "Clean yet expressive outlines. Sharp angular facets with precise borders."
    Finish: "Handcrafted artistic finish — neither purely digital nor purely traditional"

  Typography:
    Headings: "Strong serif or bold slab font. Heavy weight to match visual density."
    Body: "Clean readable sans serif inside light-colored panels."
    Numbers: "Bold, integrated into mosaic cells as dominant visual element."
    Language: "The language should match the user's prompt language."

  Composition:
    Structure: "Symmetrical visual balance. Stained-glass panel system."
    Fragmentation: "Fragmented polygonal shapes — triangles, hexagons, irregular polygons."
    Lighting: "High contrast lighting from upper-left. Bright facets vs deep shadow facets."
    Layering: "Multiple geometry layers creating depth — foreground cells, midground, background wash."

Layout Variations:
  - Type: "Central Medallion"
    Design: "Large central hexagonal or circular panel with primary subject. Surrounding polygonal cells contain supporting data. Deep blue border panels frame the composition like a cathedral window."

  - Type: "Triptych Panels"
    Design: "Three major vertical panels separated by thick dark borders. Each panel: a different warm-to-cool color gradient. Data point as large typographic anchor in each panel center."

  - Type: "Ascending Pyramid"
    Design: "Bottom-heavy composition of large mosaic cells tapering to apex. Data flows upward — foundation facts at base, key conclusions at top. Each tier uses progressively warmer hues."

  - Type: "Radial Burst"
    Design: "Central data point with radiating triangular facets spreading outward. Warm oranges near center, deep blues at edges. Supporting data in outer ring cells."

Design Rules:
  - "Angular Precision: All shapes use sharp, deliberate edges — no soft curves except where simulating light."
  - "Color Discipline: Deep blue and warm orange must appear in roughly equal visual weight on each layout."
  - "Texture Authenticity: Surface must always suggest handcrafted medium — brush strokes or wood grain visible."
  - "Border Structure: Thick dark outlines (2–4pt) separate all mosaic cells. No borderless touching panels."
  - "Light Logic: Consistent upper-left light source. Bright facets face up-left, dark facets face down-right."
```

## Compact Variant

```
A modern geometric mosaic art style with a cubist influence, featuring fragmented polygonal shapes arranged in a stained-glass composition. Strong emphasis on sharp edges, angular facets, and layered geometry. Rich, saturated color palette dominated by deep blues, warm oranges, amber, ochre, and muted neutrals, balanced with high contrast lighting. Textured painterly surface resembling acrylic or oil paint on wood, with visible brush strokes and subtle grain. Clean yet expressive outlines, symmetrical visual balance, and a decorative illustrative feel. Contemporary abstract realism with a handcrafted, artistic finish.
```

## Use Case

Visually rich infographic with decorative art appeal. Ideal for:
- Cultural, historical, or architectural subjects
- Data with natural groupings (3–6 major categories)
- Audiences who appreciate fine art and craft aesthetics
- Premium editorial, museum, or educational publications

**Orientation:** Portrait — vertical stained-glass panel flow reads naturally top to bottom.
**Detail Level:** Standard — balanced complexity for most content types.
