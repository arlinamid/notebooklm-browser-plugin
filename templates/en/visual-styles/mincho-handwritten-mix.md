# Mincho + Handwritten Mix

**Source:** Kawai
**Format:** Slide Deck
**Difficulty:** Intermediate

## Prompt

```
# presentation_design_spec_mincho_handwritten.yaml
# Style: Mincho + Handwritten Mix
# Concept: "Formal Meets Casual — The Beauty of Typographic Contrast"

Global Design Settings:
  Tone: "Stylish, sophisticated yet approachable, fashion-editorial with warmth, typographic drama"

  Color Palette:
    Base: "#FFFEF0 (warm cream — elegant paper feel)"
    Text:
      Primary: "#1A1A1A (soft black — ink, not harsh)"
      Secondary: "#5C5C5C (mid gray — secondary text)"
    Accent:
      - "#C8A96E (warm gold — for mincho headlines and decorative rules)"
      - "#E8E0D4 (pale beige — card backgrounds and dividers)"
    Optional: "One fashion-forward accent per deck theme: dusty rose #D4A5A5, sage green #9CAF88, or slate blue #8FA0B8."

  Typography:
    Mincho: "Elegant, thin-stroked serif typeface (Noto Serif, Playfair Display). Used for main headlines — formal, structured, precise."
    Handwritten: "Casual, organic handwriting-style font for annotations, labels, and secondary captions. Feels personal and spontaneous."
    Contrast: "The visual drama comes from placing formal Mincho headlines directly beside or over handwritten casual elements."
    Body: "Clean, legible sans serif for body text — a neutral bridge between the two expressive styles."
    Language: "The language should be what users said in the prompt."

  Common Layout Rules:
    TypographyAsDesign: "Headlines are laid out as design elements — large, thin, with generous white space around them."
    Stickers: "Handwritten text appears as sticker overlays, torn-paper labels, or loose annotations — slightly tilted (±3–8°)."
    Photos: "Fashion or lifestyle photography — editorial feel, soft natural light or blown-out white backgrounds."
    WhiteSpace: "Generous — the warm cream background shows through extensively. Never dense or crowded."

Layout Variations:
  - Type: "Magazine Spread"
    Design: "Cream background. Large Mincho-style headline in the upper two-thirds — thin, elegant, dramatic scale. A fashion lifestyle photo positioned asymmetrically. Handwritten caption or sticker annotation overlaid on or near the photo."

  - Type: "Side-by-Side Contrast"
    Design: "Left panel: formal Mincho headline with clean spacing and a gold rule. Right panel: the same content echoed as a casual handwritten version — demonstrating the style's core typographic contrast directly."

  - Type: "Sticker Board"
    Design: "Cream background. Multiple overlapping cards and sticker elements in varying sizes, each slightly rotated. Some cards use formal serif type, others use handwritten text. Feels like a creative mood board or pinboard."

  - Type: "Photo with Annotations"
    Design: "A soft, editorial-quality lifestyle photo fills 60% of the slide. Handwritten annotations with arrows pointing to details are placed over and around it. A formal Mincho title sits in the clean cream margin."

  - Type: "Editorial Column"
    Design: "Three columns on cream background. Left: large formal Mincho number or title. Center: editorial photo with a torn or deckle-edge crop. Right: handwritten-style body text and annotations."

  - Type: "Minimal Quote"
    Design: "Cream canvas. One short elegant quote in large, thin Mincho type centered with generous margins. Attribution in handwritten-style font beneath. A single thin gold horizontal rule. Nothing else."

  - Type: "Polaroid Gallery"
    Design: "3–4 polaroid-style photo frames (white border, slight subtle shadow) scattered on cream background. Each has a handwritten caption below, as if written on the polaroid white border area. Slightly overlapping, slightly rotated."

Design Rules:
  - "Typography Contract: Formal Mincho and casual handwritten must BOTH appear on every slide — the contrast is the design."
  - "Color Warmth: All colors are warm — cream, gold, soft beige. No cool-toned colors unless it is the chosen deck accent."
  - "Photo Style: Always editorial or lifestyle feel — never stock, never corporate. Soft light, natural setting, fashion context."
  - "Sticker Placement: Handwritten stickers and annotations are never perfectly horizontal — always ±3–8° rotation for organic feel."
  - "No Bold Blacks: Use soft black (#1A1A1A) not harsh pure #000000. This is a warm, sophisticated palette."
  - "Generation: Generate slides that celebrate typographic contrast. Every slide must contain both a formal serif headline and a casual handwritten element. Maintain warm cream palette and editorial photography style throughout."
```
