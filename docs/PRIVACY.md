# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-24

This Privacy Policy describes how Prompt Architect for NotebookLM ("the Extension", "we", "us", or "our") handles your data.

## 1. Data Collection
We respect your privacy. **Prompt Architect for NotebookLM does not collect, transmit, distribute, or sell your personal data.** 

## 2. Local Storage
The Extension requires the `storage` permission to save your preferences and custom-created prompt templates. 
- All data is saved strictly to your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`).
- Your data never leaves your device and is never sent to any external servers.

## 3. Host Permissions
The Extension requests permission to read and change data on `notebooklm.google.com`.
- This permission is used exclusively for the core functionality of the Extension: to inject the prompt template selector into the NotebookLM user interface and to insert selected text into the chat and studio text inputs.
- The Extension does not read, intercept, or log your conversations, documents, or interactions within NotebookLM.

## 4. Analytics and Tracking
The Extension does not use any third-party analytics, tracking scripts, or remote code. It operates entirely offline and locally within your browser.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. 

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue in the [GitHub Repository](https://github.com/arlinamid/notebooklm-browser-plugin/issues).
